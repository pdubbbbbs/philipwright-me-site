About

Philip S Wright is a security and privacy tools developer based in Toluca Lake, California. As founder of Always Under, Inc., he creates open source solutions that empower users to take control of their digital security.

With a focus on practical, hardware-level security implementations, Philip develops tools that bridge the gap between enterprise-grade protection and accessible, user-friendly software. His projects span VPN infrastructure, Bluetooth security hardening, and authentication management.

All projects are open source and available on GitHub, reflecting a commitment to transparency and community-driven development in the security space.

Background

The Operational Foundation

Before pivoting to cybersecurity, I spent over a decade as a Line Producer and Production Manager in the entertainment industry. I managed complex commercial productions for Fortune 500 brands — Apple, Microsoft, General Motors, Toyota, Verizon, and 30+ others — overseeing multi-million dollar budgets, leading cross-functional teams of up to 80 people, and delivering under zero-tolerance deadlines.

That career taught me how to manage risk, control costs, navigate compliance, and lead under pressure. Those same principles now drive how I build and scale BlueGuard.
